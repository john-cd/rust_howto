## Visualization

Graphic and visualization for Robotics.

`kiss3d` - Keep it simple, stupid 3D graphics engine for Rust.
`urdf-viz` - URDF visualizer
`rerun` - A logging SDK and visualizer for computer vision and robotics

### `kiss3d`

### `urdf-viz`
